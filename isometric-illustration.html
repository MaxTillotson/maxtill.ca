Isometric Illustration

Project Brief

The concept behind this project was to create a process in the style of a Isometric Illustration. Isometric Illustrations have equal dimensions and have no vanishing points.

Sketches

The process I wanted to create was the creation of juice. Adding my own twist to it, the scene of the process takes place on ‘Juice Island’.
